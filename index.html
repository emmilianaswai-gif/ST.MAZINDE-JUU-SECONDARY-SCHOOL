About our school

St. Mary's Mazinde juu Secondary school is commited to academic excellence,discipline, and moral values. discipline, and moral values.

St. Mary's Mazinde juu secondary school is a girl boarding school in the Mountain of Notheastern Tanzania in Eastern Africa. Owned by catholic Dioces of Tanga, and run by loczl order of sisters,it is affiliated with St. Paul's Abbey, a Bennedictine monastery in Newton ,New jersey.Snce 28th February 1998,Mazinde juu has been a place where many students have had opportunity to learn and dicover themselves.I congratulate you all who have gone through this institution. Our vision is to provide Quality Education for Tanzania Girls and develop them Morally and Spiritually to be a Good Force in any society that Live in.
